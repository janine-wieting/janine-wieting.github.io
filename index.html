About Me

Hi, my name is Janine and I am a Berlin based aspiring UX/UI Designer. I started my career path with the studies of business and economics and jumped into the world of market research after that. During my path in this field I became a project manager. I developed curiosity for humans and their desires and motivations, as well as needs - but basically everything that’s effecting humans and their behavior. That’s why I went on as a Trainee in Customer Research and Consultancy.

I changed my path to UX/UI Design because of the desire to work still structured and research based, but also more creative, visual and technology driven.

In my free time you can find me making music, having walks or bike tours in nature and collecting fun and pretty stuff at flea markets.
